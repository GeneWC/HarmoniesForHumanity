Justin Vo

Justin Vo is a student at Tomball Memorial High School and plays in the "Roarin' Blue Band" Wind Symphony. Justin is a frequent TMEA All State Oboist, and has performed two years consecutively. Through these experiences, Justin realized the lasting impact music has on people and wanted to share his passion to the underprivleged and those in need. Aside from playing Oboe, Justin also plays Violin and Piano at a high level.
